About Me

I'm a Product Manager with 4 years of experience building products that matter. My journey spans government tech, fintech, AI platforms, and sustainability solutions—always with a focus on data-driven decision making and user-centric design.

Currently, I'm leading product delivery at Zenika for GIC's performance management platform, where I've improved data integrity, optimized observability, and automated workflows. My unique strength lies in bridging technical complexity with business outcomes—I speak both the language of engineers and stakeholders.

What sets me apart? I combine deep technical certifications (SQL, Python) with proven enterprise delivery experience. I've conducted user research with 1,000+ users, achieved 100% compliance across government agencies, and built products from MVP to scale. I'm not just managing products—I'm building the technical infrastructure and data platforms that power them.

My Journey

My path to product management wasn't linear—it was driven by curiosity about how technology can solve real problems. I was extremely interested in building 0 to 1 products based on user needs. Starting with a foundation in business and data analytics, I quickly realized that the best products come from understanding both the technical possibilities and the human needs behind them.

At GIC, I've learned what it takes to deliver enterprise-grade features with detailed testing. At Affinidi, I discovered the power of deep user research in building products that people actually want. And through my startup experience with GreenChain, I've experienced the full lifecycle—from ideation to fundraising to building.
